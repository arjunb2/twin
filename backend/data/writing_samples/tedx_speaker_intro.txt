Starting as a camera assistant in Thiruvananthapuram to becoming one of Malayalam cinema's most influential cinematographers, Sujith Vasudev has painted stories in light and shadow in films like Drishyam, Memories, 7th Day and Lucifer. His works did not just capture scenes, but captured feeling.
At TEDxCUSAT GENESIS, he steps out from behind the lens to reflect on beginnings, how ideas are born, how a single thought evolves into something that moves us, and how every frame we see started long before the camera rolled.
This is not just about cinema. It is about the first spark, the first choice, the genesis of vision itself and the courage to share it.
