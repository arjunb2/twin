Latest Space Highlights | March 2026

ISRO TESTS CE-20 CRYOGENIC ENGINE
ISRO recently carried out a successful hot test of the CE-20 cryogenic engine, reaching a thrust of around 22 tonnes.
Cryogenic engines are complex to work with, but they offer higher efficiency, especially for missions that need to carry heavier payloads. This test is another step towards improving India's heavy-lift capabilities.

WHAT'S NEXT AFTER CHANDRAYAAN-3?
After the success of Chandrayaan-3, ISRO is already planning ahead.
Future missions like Chandrayaan-4 and 5 are expected to focus on more advanced objectives, including the possibility of bringing lunar samples back to Earth and deploying heavier rovers for extended missions.

DRAGONFLY: A MISSION TO TITAN
NASA has started building Dragonfly, a rotorcraft designed to explore Titan.
Unlike traditional rovers, Dragonfly will be able to fly between locations, making exploration more flexible. Titan's complex chemistry makes it an interesting place to study the origins of life.

SPACE FACT OF THE ISSUE
Titan has rivers and lakes, but they are made of liquid methane instead of water. It is one of the most Earth-like environments in the solar system, despite being extremely different.
